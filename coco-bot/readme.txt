The first repository for chatbot conversational components. Create advanced chatbots and connect them to your website. Use existing components or create new ones using Dialogflow, MS bot framework, Rasa and more.

== Description ==

Our growing hub of shareable, modular CoCos is the first of its kind and represents a paradigm shift in how we design and interact with conversational AI.

CoCos incorporated into your bot framework make it flexible, maintain contextual flow throughout conversations and deliver the goals you require faster.

You can also customize your CoCos or repurpose them to cut down development costs, improve the quality of conversations, and facilitate faster deployment.

Major features in CoCoHub Wordpress plugin include:

* Connect any component/bot to your website.
* Build your bot on any of the popular platforms any add it to your website using the component interface.
* Mix and match existing components to build your bot, customize them to give it personality.

== Installation ==

Install and activate the plugin.
Under the plugin settings set your bot name and set your bot component_url from cocohub.ai

Use the shortcode [cocobot] to add it to your page
